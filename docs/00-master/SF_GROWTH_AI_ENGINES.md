# SF Growth AI — Engines

Version: 1.0

---

# Purpose

This document defines the intelligence engines that power SF Growth AI.

Each engine has one clear responsibility.

---

# 1. Samuel AI™

Main conversational interface.

It talks to the user, explains recommendations and coordinates the platform.

---

# 2. Business DNA™

Stores the identity of the company.

It defines:

- Mission
- Vision
- Values
- Brand personality
- Tone of voice
- Customer promise
- Competitive philosophy

---

# 3. Business Twin™

Stores the current state of the company.

It includes:

- Services
- Products
- Customers
- Channels
- Sales
- Marketing
- Competitors
- Financial data
- Business history

---

# 4. Growth Memory™

Stores everything that happened over time.

It remembers:

- Recommendations
- Actions
- Campaigns
- Results
- Conversations
- Score changes

---

# 5. Research Engine™

Collects external information.

Sources:

- Website
- Google Business
- Social media
- Competitors
- Market trends
- SEO data

---

# 6. Growth Score Engine™

Calculates business maturity from 0 to 1000.

It evaluates:

- Brand
- Digital presence
- Google visibility
- Content
- Sales
- Customer experience
- Automation
- Data

---

# 7. Decision Engine™

Chooses the next best action.

It prioritizes actions based on:

- Impact
- Urgency
- Effort
- Business goal
- Growth Score gap

---

# 8. Opportunity Engine™

Detects opportunities before the user asks.

Examples:

- Seasonal campaigns
- Competitor weakness
- Search trends
- Customer retention opportunities

---

# 9. Prediction Engine™

Simulates possible outcomes.

Examples:

- What happens if ad budget increases?
- What happens if prices change?
- What happens if content frequency improves?

---

# 10. Execution Engine™

Turns decisions into assets.

It creates:

- Posts
- Reels scripts
- Ads
- Campaigns
- Flyers
- Emails
- WhatsApp messages
- Landing page copy

---

# 11. Automation Engine™

Executes approved tasks through integrations.

Examples:

- Schedule posts
- Send emails
- Create tasks
- Update CRM
- Generate reports

---

# 12. Collective Intelligence™

Learns from anonymized patterns across businesses.

It helps compare companies with similar profiles while protecting privacy.

---

# Golden Rule

The user should never see complexity.

The user sees:

- Clear diagnosis
- Next best action
- Execution support
- Progress
