# SF Growth AI — 3 Year Vision

Version: 1.0

---

# Vision

In 3 years, SF Growth AI will be the intelligent operating system for small and medium businesses.

The platform will not only answer questions.

It will understand the business, monitor the market, detect opportunities, recommend actions, execute tasks and track results.

---

# Core Product

SF Growth AI will work as an AI Executive Board for every company.

The user will interact mainly with Samuel AI™, but internally the platform will coordinate multiple executives, engines and data sources.

---

# Main User Experience

The entrepreneur opens the platform and sees:

1. Business health
2. Growth Score
3. Next best action
4. Opportunities detected
5. Risks detected
6. Daily missions
7. Monthly goals
8. Samuel AI conversation

The user should always know what to do next.
