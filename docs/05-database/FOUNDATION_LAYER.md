# Foundation Layer

Version 1.0

---

## Objective

The Foundation Layer represents the identity of the entire platform.

Nothing in SF Growth AI exists without this layer.

---

## Tables

### users

Platform users.

---

### companies

Companies registered in the platform.

---

### company_members

Relationship between users and companies.

One company can have multiple users.

One user can belong to multiple companies.

---

## Rules

Every company belongs to at least one user.

Every Business Twin belongs to one company.

Every Executive AI works inside one company.
