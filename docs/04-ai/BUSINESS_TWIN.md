# Business Twin™

Version: 1.0

---

# Definition

The Business Twin™ is the digital representation of a company.

It stores everything necessary for Samuel AI to completely understand how the business works.

The objective is that the AI never analyzes a company based only on isolated information.

Instead, it understands the complete business.

---

# Purpose

Transform data into business intelligence.

Every recommendation generated by Samuel AI must use the Business Twin as its primary source of truth.
