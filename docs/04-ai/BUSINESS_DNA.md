# Business DNA™

Version: 1.0

---

# Definition

The Business DNA™ represents the immutable identity of a company.

While the Business Twin changes every day, the Business DNA changes very little over time.

It defines how the company thinks, communicates and creates value.

---

# Purpose

Allow Samuel AI to understand the personality of a business instead of only its data.
