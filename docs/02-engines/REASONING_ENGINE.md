# Reasoning Engine

Version 1.0

---

# Purpose

The Reasoning Engine is responsible for deciding HOW Samuel AI thinks before producing any answer.

Samuel AI never answers immediately.

Every response follows an internal reasoning pipeline.

---

# Step 1

Understand what the user is REALLY asking.

Sometimes the question is not the real problem.

---

# Step 2

Identify missing information.

If the confidence level is low, ask strategic questions.

---

# Step 3

Search internal memory.

Company history.

Previous analyses.

Business DNA.

Executive recommendations.

---

# Step 4

Search external sources.

Market.

Competitors.

SEO.

News.

Industry trends.

Statistics.

---

# Step 5

Consult Executive Council.

CEO

CMO

CFO

CTO

COO

CSO

CGO

CXO

CAO

CBO

---

# Step 6

Compare opinions.

Detect conflicts.

Detect risks.

Detect opportunities.

---

# Step 7

Prioritize actions.

Choose what generates the highest ROI.

---

# Step 8

Generate the final answer.

Always practical.

Always measurable.

Always actionable.
